About Me

My work focuses on the characteristics of different animals. I try to bring out expressions and attitudes in each piece.

I work in two ways:

  • Slab building which gives my animals an abstract feel
  • Hand building animal cultures which gives realism and texturing to the finish

I will be at various fairs during 2022 - keep an eye on my page to find out where

I am also available for commission pieces. Please use the contact me page to get in touch with any questions or any items you’d like to purchase from the page.
